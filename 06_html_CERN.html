CERN Information

About CERN

CERN is a European research organization.
It is one of the world's largest physics laboratories.
It studies particles and the universe.

Important Work

CERN invented the World Wide Web (WWW).
It helps scientists understand how the universe works.
It uses advanced machines like the Large Hadron Collider.

Features

Advanced scientific research.
High energy physics experiments.
Global collaboration with scientists.
Innovation in technology.

Importance

CERN helps in scientific discoveries.
It improves technology and research.
It contributes to global knowledge.
