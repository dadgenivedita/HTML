Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is one of the world's leading IT services, consulting, and business solutions companies. It is a part of the Tata Group, one of India's most respected business organizations.

Founded in 1968, TCS has grown into a global company with operations in more than 50 countries. The company helps businesses improve efficiency, innovation, and growth through advanced technology solutions.

Company Overview

TCS provides software development, cloud computing, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, and IT consulting services to clients worldwide.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1968
  • Headquarters: Mumbai, India
  • Parent Company: Tata Group
  • Employees: Over 600,000+
  • Global Presence: 50+ Countries
  • Industry: Information Technology

Achievements
